Flowstate: The FPV Drone Documentary takes viewers into the heart of FPV (First-Person View) drone racing, exploring the intense focus and freedom that define the sport. Through the eyes of skilled pilots, the film showcases the mental "flow state" that allows them to perform at their peak, where instinct and precision guide every move. The documentary offers a behind-the-scenes look at drone competitions, highlighting the dedication and creativity of pilots, from global racers to hobbyists. It blends breathtaking aerial footage with moments of reflection, celebrating the artistry and thrill of FPV flying.
